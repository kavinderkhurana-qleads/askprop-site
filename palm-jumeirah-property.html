Common questions

Palm Jumeirah — buyer questions answered

Is Palm Jumeirah freehold for foreign buyers?
Yes. Palm Jumeirah is a designated freehold area, so foreign nationals of any nationality can buy, own, sell and inherit property with full DLD-registered title.
What rental yield does Palm Jumeirah offer?
Gross yields in Palm Jumeirah run to roughly 5.4% (indicative, Jan–Jun 2026 data); smaller units typically yield highest. AiSHA confirms the live figure for a specific unit.
Can I get a UAE Golden Visa through Palm Jumeirah property?
Yes. Any property at AED 2M+ qualifies for the 10-year UAE Golden Visa — ready or off-plan (AED 2M paid to DLD), with a mortgage allowed if your equity is at least AED 2M. It covers your spouse and children.
What are the total buying costs in Palm Jumeirah?
Budget roughly 7% on top of the price: DLD registration 4%, agency 2% + VAT, trustee and admin fees, plus annual service charges. Since 2026 these closing costs are paid in cash, not financed. Your specialist confirms exact figures.
